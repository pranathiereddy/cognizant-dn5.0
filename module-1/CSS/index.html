Community Bulletin

Latest news from around the community

The city council has announced a series of community events this summer aimed at bringing residents together. From music nights to food festivals, there is something for everyone to enjoy in the coming months.

Local volunteers are being requested to assist with setup and coordination of these events. If you are interested in helping out, please contact the community office before the end of this month.

The annual sports day will feature new activities this year including a fun relay race and a cricket match open to all age groups. Registration is completely free and families are encouraged to participate together.

The art exhibition will showcase work from over 30 local artists. Paintings, sculptures, and digital art will all be on display. Entry is open to the public and refreshments will be provided by local vendors.
